Automated Retinal Screening and Diagnosis

Retinal diseases like Diabetic Retinopathy, AMD, and Glaucoma are major health concerns that can lead to vision loss if undiagnosed. Manual diagnosis is time-consuming and prone to inconsistencies, highlighting the need for an automated, efficient detection system. This project developed a deep learning model for classifying 11 retinal disease categories, aiming to improve early detection and treatment outcomes. The system enhances diagnostic accuracy by identifying disease-specific patterns in retinal images, even at early stages.
